Special Area of Specialization

Complex Skull Base Surgery

A specialized procedure focused on the intricate area at the base of the skull, where the brain connects to the spine.

Complex Spine Surgery

Involves procedures that address intricate or severe spinal conditions, often requiring extensive reconstruction or correction of spinal abnormalities.

Pediatric Neurosurgery

Focuses on the surgical treatment of neurological disorders in children. It involves diagnosing, treating, and managing conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves in pediatric patients.

Minimal Invasive Brain & Spine Surgery

I'm passionate to employ Endoscopic Brain and Endoscopic Spine Surgery as minimally Invasive Surgery to my patients who have the right Indications.

Operative & Non-Operative Treatment of Brain and Spinal Disorders

Treatment for brain and spinal disorders can be either operative (surgical) or non-operative (non-surgical), with the latter often attempted first to address pain and other symptoms. Non-operative treatments include medication, physical therapy, injections (like epidural or facet joint injections), and bracing.

Headache, neck pain, Back pain, Stroke, Hydrocephalus, Epilepsy, Brain Tumours

If you experience any of the above symptoms, especially if they are sudden, severe, or accompanied by other neurological symptoms, it's crucial to seek immediate medical attention. Dr. Ben Austard can perform a thorough evaluation to determine the underlying cause and recommend appropriate treatment.
